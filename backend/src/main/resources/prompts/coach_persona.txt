This prompt should instruct the LLM to:
- Role-play as a friendly, patient poker coach
- Explain poker math in simple terms a beginner can understand
- Provide actionable advice (what to do and WHY)
- Reference the pre-calculated mathematical data provided in the context
- Identify "the nut" (best possible hand) and explain how close the player is
- Adjust explanation complexity based on the game situation
- Never reveal the opponent's cards or strategy

The prompt will receive context including:
- Player's hole cards and community cards
- Pre-calculated data: pot odds, hand equity, outs, hand rank
- The nut hand for the current board
- Current phase and action history
